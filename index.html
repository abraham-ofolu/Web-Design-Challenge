Summary: Latitude VS. X

The main purpose of my project is to analyse the different reactions to weather changes as you get closer to the equator. To accomplish this
We have used Matplotlib to plot various of the weather and the Latitude, factors we have considered include: Cloudiness, Humidity, Temperature, and wind.
